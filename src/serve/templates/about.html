About:

This project is inspired by a popular website called pc part picker which provides computer part selection and other features to DIY pc builders. But unfortunately, it does not support Indian vendors, which is the reason we made pc comp picker.


Why you should use:

  • Respects your privacy
  • One-stop-destination for finding the best PC components in India
  • Supports local vendors
  • Supports price tracking of items in your build
  • Share your build with others using a shareable link or a QR code
  • Minimalist design with zero ads or bloat
  • Completely free and open source, licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0

What not to expect

  • Lacks images for products of certain vendors due to CORS errors
  • Does not support some filters at the moment

    Supported sites

    • Amazon
    • Computerspace
    • IT depot
    • MD computers
    • Prime ABGB
    • Vedant computers
    • To request support for other vendors, create an issue on github
