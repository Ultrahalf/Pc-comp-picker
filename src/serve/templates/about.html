About:

This project is a clone of a popular website called pc parts picker. Unfortunately it does not support Indian vendors.


Why should you use

  • Privacy respecting.
  • Support for many website all in one place.
  • Supports local vendors.
  • Supports price tracking.
  • Sharable links and QR code.
  • Minimalistic design/ Ads free.
  • Open source(licensed under GNU Affero General Public License v3.0)

What not to expect

  • Doesn not support images for some website due to COR
  • Does not support many filter at the moment.
  • Wishlist will be deleted after 3 months of their creation.
